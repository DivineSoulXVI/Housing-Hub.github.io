Concept of our website.

The concept of our website is a housing website. We picked this topic as most of us are international students and have gone through some difficulty with finding a place to stay when coming to The Netherlands. Mainly because of not knowing where to start looking. So we have made this website in hopes of making it easier for international students coming to study in Eindhoven to find a place to stay. We are mainly going to focus on housing in Eindhoven.

Collab with other housing websites.

For our project we have decided to collab with 4 other housing websites, those being:
- FriendlyHousing
- Holland2Stay
- Kamernet
- Vestide
